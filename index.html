Introduction

Welcome to the Social Protocol Directive, a Web3 Accelerator Program, where we empower founders to tackle the most pressing societal challenges through groundbreaking technology solutions. Our program is focused on driving innovation in two key categories: societal governance and societal awareness.

We are committed to fostering innovation through the Open World Design. This design, envisioned and built on top of blockchain technology, brings the power of network consensus to society, where everyone can equally prove their rights.

Web3 technology meets the need for secure, verified statements from all parties in society. In this era of AI-driven innovation, traditional intellectual property frameworks are no longer equipped to address the complexities of provenance and authenticity.

It's a high-level architecture designed for our upcoming technological era, providing a framework for transparent and accountable solutions.

Societal Governance

In today's rapidly evolving world, navigating external conflicts is paramount for individuals, corporations, and governments alike. That's why we're committed to supporting ventures that aim to facilitate resolution in this area, helping parties settle the past to pave the way for a brighter future. Whether it's disputes stemming from historical grievances, economic inequalities, or geopolitical tensions, we believe that technology can play a pivotal role in fostering reconciliation and sustainable peace.

Through our Empowered Society Accelerator Program, we not only participate in applicant projects but also facilitate fundraising and go-to-market strategy to ensure their success. Founders will have the opportunity to develop and scale proven source Web3 applications that address societal governance issues head-on. By leveraging the Open World Protocol, blockchain technology, decentralized governance frameworks, and smart contract solutions, our entrepreneurs will empower stakeholders to engage in transparent, accountable, and consensus-driven decision-making processes.

Societal Awareness

True societal transformation begins from within, which is why we're equally dedicated to supporting ventures that focus on societal awareness issues. In a world where internal conflicts such as mental health challenges, identity crises, and existential dilemmas are becoming increasingly prevalent, it's imperative that we equip individuals, corporations, and governments with the tools they need to navigate these complexities with resilience and empathy.

Through the Empowered Society Accelerator Program, we facilitate fundraising and go-to-market strategy for ventures that aim to address societal awareness issues. Founders will have the opportunity to develop and scale proven source Web3 applications that empower individuals to evolve and grow personally and professionally. Leveraging the Open World Protocol, decentralized identity solutions, augmented reality, gamification, and more, our entrepreneurs will have the support they need to bring their vision to life.
