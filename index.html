How We Reward

Our mission is to serve society with maximum value (first) and compensate contributors, aligned to market standards (second) to ultimately celebrate positive change with bonus rewards for all Alliance members (third). To enable a fast-moving environment we have holistically designed a reward system that considers skill, effort and quality to establish a clear reward perspective for the Alliance members and their contributions.

1. Join

Join the Unified Tech Alliance as Individual or Organsation.

2. Participate

UTA Members have access to a list of objectives (to form a taskforce group), existing projects (to support existing groups) to decide where members will participate. Participation is completely managed through DAOs and an Agreement where skills, time, goals and rewards are weighted for future rewarding.

3. Generate

Participating UTA Members are grouped into DAOs where objectives and necessary skillsets are a match. The UTA measures objectives and results and rewards accordingly on a Monthly basis.

Weighted Rewards

Based on weighted contracted work/skills, spent time, delivered results, reached objectives. Rewards are fairly distributed based on Fullfilment (work), Quality (result) and Vetting (time).

Our Essential Projects

With the Unified Architecture Consensus we intend to empower communities through accessible and open-source platforms, fostering social inclusivity in our shared digital landscape. The essential projects are defined to reflect the current vision on Decentralized Autonomous Cloud.

the Universal Web

The Universal Web is a (Decentralized Autonomous Cloud) community-driven innovation, where individuals play a pivotal role in shaping a decentralized ecosystem. Here, people have the opportunity to contribute their computing, storage, or transmission abilities to the network, fostering a collaborative environment that thrives on diversity and inclusivity. Through a consensus protocol, contributions are rewarded, ensuring fairness and transparency. This approach not only makes the network elastic and adaptable to varying demands but also economically viable and environmentally friendly. The Universal Web celebrates the power of collective action and the transformative potential of decentralized technologies to create a more sustainable and equitable future.

The Universal Web is designed to serve as a foundational building block for both smaller and larger communities within society. With a focus on interconnectedness, it aims to foster collaboration and cohesion, empowering communities to thrive at every scale.

the Unified Records

A decentralized content storage network that enables the Universal Web to provide Personal Data Vault, Replicated Ledgers and a general decentralized Cloud Storage network.

the Universal Coin

Enabling every governed community run by the Universal Web to utilize it's own Coin to incentivice and tax network and economical activity.

the heartXchange

An AI driven reward platform based on social sustainability. The future of capitalism.
