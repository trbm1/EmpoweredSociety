Our Essential Projects

With the Unified Architecture Consensus we intend to empower communities through accessible and open-source platforms, fostering social inclusivity in our shared digital landscape.

the Universal Web

The Universal Web is a (Decentralized Autonomous Cloud) community-driven innovation, where individuals play a pivotal role in shaping a decentralized ecosystem. Here, people have the opportunity to contribute their computing, storage, or transmission abilities to the network, fostering a collaborative environment that thrives on diversity and inclusivity. Through a consensus protocol, contributions are rewarded, ensuring fairness and transparency. This approach not only makes the network elastic and adaptable to varying demands but also economically viable and environmentally friendly. The Proxy celebrates the power of collective action and the transformative potential of decentralized technologies to create a more sustainable and equitable future.

The Universal Web is designed to serve as a foundational building block for both smaller and larger communities within society. With a focus on interconnectedness, it aims to foster collaboration and cohesion, empowering communities to thrive at every scale.

the Unified Records

A decentralized content storage network that enables the Universal Web to provide Personal Data Vault, Replicated Ledgers and a general decentralized Cloud Storage network.

the Universal Coin

Enabling every governed community run by the Universal Web to utilize it's own Coin to incentivice and tax network and economical activity.

the heartXchange

An AI driven reward platform based on social sustainability. The future of capitalism.
